We offer the Best Services

Emergency Medicine

We offer comprehensive emergency services and the highest level of care to any patient with any condition at multiple locations.We have designated centers for cardiac emergencies, stroke and trauma.The goal of emergency and trauma treatment is to stabilize patients and help them through the acute phase of their illness or injury.We use advanced diagnostic testing and imaging services along with emergency care specialists for evaluation and treatment of complicated medical conditions. When timely expert care is needed,Our Emergency Medicine services offers a full range of specialists, technologies and systems.Our emergency specialists will create individualized diagnostic plans to ensure the highest quality of care.

Imaging Services

We offer the latest advances in diagnostic imaging, a skilled team of caring specialists and comprehensive services that are fully coordinated with your medical care. Your care is backed by:A strong commitment to quality, safety and satisfaction,State-of-the-art technology in the hands of highly trained imaging specialists and An exceptional imaging experience that earned “5-star” satisfaction scores in patient surveys.Your imaging exam will be performed by healthcare professionals with skill and training in diagnostic imaging. Our imaging specialists include:Board-certified radiologists,Licensed radiologic technologists, including those with sub-specialization in CT, MRI and other diagnostic imaging techniques Registered nurses with additional credentials in a critical care subspecialty. Our diagnostic imaging technologists have many years of experience in the field.

Laboratory Services

Our lab specialists conduct routine tests ordered by your physician in conjunction with annual physicals as well as testing requested for the complex diagnosis or monitoring of medical conditions.The lab facilities are upto date with great equipments for testing of various diseases and is inbuilt with the best scanning equipments.Our trained professionals are certified with the best usage of equipments and you will be of great care under them.Diagnosis of cancer,thyroid,blood test and much more are done at ease and the results are provided faster than any other medical labarotories.

Pain Management

Pain management specialists work to relieve the underlying causes of your pain, so that you can start to live more comfortably. Your personalized treatment plan draws from a broad range of approaches to pain management—from the very latest medical techniques to proven holistic therapies. Destiny HealthCare Pain Management services offer a number of medical and interventional treatments that can help most individuals manage their pain successfully.We also offer exceptional, innovative pain management care, including the most advanced minimally invasive procedures for reducing or eliminating pain and restoring physical function.We support patients receiving obstetric or emergency care, or those treated for a variety of acute care medical needs, such as injuries.

Surgical Services

Surgery is the practice of treating diseases and disorders through incisions (surgical openings) in a patient's body that allow surgeons to remove or change parts of the body.Our Surgical Services provides the highest quality surgical care to patients, from minimally invasive procedures to more complex surgical procedures and also our Surgical Services is dedicated to serving patients' specific needs with high quality care and safe practices.Our specially trained surgeons are skilled and experienced with a wide range of procedures to ensure you have the best possible outcome from your operation.We offer the best advanced surgical care using innovations that offer greater precision,faster healing and less pain.
